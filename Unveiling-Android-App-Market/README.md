# Unveiling the Android App Market

## 📊 Overview
This project analyzes Google Play Store data to understand app market dynamics, user behavior, and factors influencing app success. The analysis includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and sentiment analysis.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Clean and preprocess the dataset  
- Analyze app distribution across categories  
- Study app ratings and user satisfaction  
- Explore installs, reviews, and popularity trends  
- Compare free vs paid apps  
- Analyze pricing strategies  
- Perform sentiment analysis on user reviews  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Most apps are highly rated (between 4.0 and 5.0)  
- The **FAMILY** category contains the highest number of apps  
- App popularity (installs) does not strongly affect ratings  
- Apps with more reviews tend to have more stable ratings  
- Paid apps show slightly higher and more consistent ratings than free apps  
- Most paid apps are priced below $5  
- Installs are concentrated in the moderate range (10K–1M)  
- User sentiment is mostly positive  
- Sentiment strongly aligns with app ratings
