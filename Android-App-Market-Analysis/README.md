
#  Android App Market Analysis

## 📌  Project Title

Unveiling the Android App Market: Analyzing Google Play Store Data

---

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes the Google Play Store dataset to uncover trends in app categories, user ratings, installs, and pricing. The goal is to understand key factors that influence app success in the Android marketplace.

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze distribution of apps across categories
* Understand relationship between ratings, installs, and pricing
* Evaluate user sentiment from reviews
* Identify factors contributing to successful apps

---

## 📊 Dataset

The dataset includes information about apps on the Google Play Store:

### Apps Dataset:

* App Name
* Category
* Rating
* Reviews
* Size
* Installs
* Price

### User Reviews Dataset:

* App Name
* Translated Review
* Sentiment (Positive / Neutral / Negative)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Majority of apps on the Play Store are free
* Categories like **Family** and **Games** dominate
* Higher installs are generally associated with better ratings
* Paid apps are fewer but tend to have higher average ratings
* User sentiment is largely positive for popular apps

---

## 📊 Results

* Identified top-performing categories
* Analyzed relationships between installs, ratings, and pricing
* Extracted insights from user sentiment data
* Provided actionable insights for app developers

## 🚀 Conclusion

The Android app market is highly competitive, with free apps dominating the ecosystem. Data analysis reveals clear patterns in app performance, helping developers make informed decisions regarding pricing, features, and targeting.
